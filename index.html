Game Guide

How to Play

Click the SPIN button to spin the wheel and get a random rank. Each rank has a different rarity and value. You can sell your items in the inventory for coins, or use coins to buy poison from the shop to increase your luck.

Rarity System

Items are ranked from most common to rarest. The rarer the item, the higher its value and the lower its drop probability.
